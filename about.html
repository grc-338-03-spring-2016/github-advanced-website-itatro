About the Maminka Girl

I am a maker of one of a kind goods using vintage fabrics, recycled and upcycled materials, ephemera and old papers. I make unique dolls, home goods, jewelry and artful cards. You can read more about my art on my blog and buy my work on my etsy shop. I love to make things, collect things and cook things! I am so happy in my studio making dolls and surrounded by my stash of vintage fabric. My family calls it my "lair".

The majority of my work consists of hand sewn favors, clothing, and dolls. My signature doll is the maminka, which is where my name comes from. In addition to dolls, I have started creating soldered jewelry using found objects, vintage fabric, and other miscellaneous materials. Many of these jewelry pieces incorporate small metal shadow boxes with a scene assembled in the center. I have also enjoyed creating brooches with plaster and hand-sculpted clay charms.When I am not creating things in my studio I am likely spending time with my family and dogs.
